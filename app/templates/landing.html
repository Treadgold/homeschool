Welcome to Life Learners

Together we are the Village!
We connect home educated families to each other, the community, and life skills through non-profit workshops, activities, playgroups, trips, and learning experiences. Our mission is to build relationships and connections through enrichment and exploration, sparking interests that turn into learning passions.

Weekly Sessions & Workshops

Life Learners offers a vibrant calendar of weekly sessions, special events, and holiday catch-ups. Our sessions include hands-on workshops, creative arts, science, outdoor adventures, and more—designed for all ages and interests. We believe in learning by doing, and our community thrives on curiosity and collaboration.

Playgroup & Little Learners

Our playgroup area is a welcoming space for mums, bubs, and little learners under 7. We offer specially designed workshops for 4–7 year olds in our 'Tinker Space', right next to the indoor playgroup. Activities include STEM, building, cooking, reading, writing, math, sensory and messy play, imagination, team games, art, and exploring the natural world.
If you need support during sessions, our coordinators and community are here to help—together we enable participation for all.
